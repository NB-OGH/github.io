Case study

Hybrid cloud file modernization

Industry: Manufacturing · Servers: 25 · Users: 200

Manufacturing client, name withheld

Challenge

More than 60 percent of company data lived on on premises file servers. People split work between network shares and SharePoint and often created duplicate versions.

Approach

  • Held planning sessions with business and IT leaders
  • Planned a phased move to SharePoint libraries with clear permissions
  • Kept key shares running during the change to avoid disruption
  • Enabled hybrid identity so users had one sign in across systems

Outcomes

  • Moved more than 60 percent of data to SharePoint and OneDrive in six months
  • Lower storage costs on old file servers
  • Better daily collaboration with Teams and SharePoint

What made it work

Keep changes small and steady, document clearly, and sequence major steps around business milestones.
