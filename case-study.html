Case study

Infrastructure refresh & sustainable operations

Industry: Creative / Marketing · Scale: 80+ endpoints · Locations: 3

Challenge

Aging systems across three offices led to inconsistent backups, patching gaps, and unclear governance, creating service risk and operational drag.

Approach

  • Consolidated backup tooling and policies
  • Introduced policy‑based patch management
  • Defined baseline configurations and runbooks
  • Created onboarding checklist and device standards

Outcomes

  • Standardized backups across offices and endpoints
  • ~25% fewer ad‑hoc support tickets through automation
  • ~40% faster new‑hire onboarding with checklists
  • Clear roadmap for staged cloud migration

What made it work

Start with low‑friction wins, document relentlessly, and make operations boring in the best way. Then sequence the bigger moves (cloud migration) with business milestones.
