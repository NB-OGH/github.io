Case study

Infrastructure refresh and sustainable operations

Industry: Creative and Marketing · Scale: 80+ endpoints · Locations: 3

Confidential client in the creative/marketing sector

Challenge

The company operated aging systems across multiple offices. Backups were inconsistent and patching varied by machine. This created service risk and frequent support tickets.

Approach

  • Standardized backup tooling and policies across sites
  • Introduced policy based patching and baseline configurations
  • Documented runbooks and a simple onboarding checklist
  • Outlined a staged plan for future cloud migration

Outcomes

  • Backups standardized across offices and endpoints
  • About a quarter fewer ad hoc support tickets through automation
  • Onboarding time for new hires cut significantly
  • Clear path for moving more workloads to the cloud

What made it work

Keep changes small and steady, document clearly, and sequence major steps around business milestones.
